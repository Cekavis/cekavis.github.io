Fast and Robust 3D Gaussian Splatting for Virtual Reality

Xuechang Tu1,2, Bernhard Kerbl1, Fernando De la Torre1,
1Carnegie Mellon University, 2Peking University
Abstract

Abstract

3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) [Kerbl et al. 2023] has quickly risen to become a highly influential method for novel-view synthesis. The high rendering performance of tile-based rasterization opens the door for many real-time applications, enabling realistic scene rendering on weaker and mobile devices. However, the original 3DGS solution faces several challenges for virtual reality (VR) applications: the 3DGS codebase readily integrates with extended reality (XR) libraries (OpenXR), but suffers from temporal artifacts (popping). While tolerable for desktop viewing, the high display resolution and immersion of VR applications for head-mounted displays (HMDs) amplify the visual artifacts, impacting the user experience.

In this work, we propose to synergize a variety of recent extensions to 3DGS and improve the VR experience. These extensions have been developed and considered in isolation; in this work, we show how they can be meaningfully combined to yield a high-quality, high-performance VR pipeline. We first use Mini-Splatting [Fang and Wang 2024] to raise rendering frame rates. Next, we employ StopThePop [Radl et al. 2024], to address temporal popping artifacts. The off-axis projection and field-of-view found in HMDs can give rise to particularly unsightly ghosting; we address it using Optimal Projection for 3DGS [Huang et al. 2024]. Finally, to ensure a target framerate of >72 FPS at HMD-native resolution, we propose a simple foveated rendering mechanism.

Performance

Our solution achieves the target frame rate of 72 FPS (i.e., less than 14 ms stereo) in the scenes from MipNeRF360 and Tanks&Temples, at Meta Quest 3 native resolution on an NVIDIA RTX 4090. The table below shows how performance changes as we introduce the suggested features.

3DGS Mini-Splatting + StopThePop + Optimal Projection + Foveated Rendering
Truck 9.8 ms 3.7 ms 15.1 ms 15.1 ms 10.8 ms
Garden 14.4 ms 4.0 ms 13.6 ms 13.6 ms 11.5 ms
Room 10.6 ms 4.7 ms 19.9 ms 19.9 ms 13.0 ms
